Concept: Talk about Weaknesses
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence structure:
"My weakness is [weakness], so I am working on [action]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. Your weakness is public speaking.

Share what you are doing to improve.
2. Your weakness is time management.

Share what you are doing to improve.
3. Your weakness is technical skills.

Share what you are doing to improve.
4. Your weakness is working under pressure.

Share what you are doing to improve.
5. Your weakness is paying attention to details.

Share what you are doing to improve.

Examples:
- "My weakness is public speaking, so I am working on building confidence."
- "My weakness is perfectionism, so I am working on accepting good-enough work."
- "My weakness is technical skills, so I am working on learning new software."
- "My weakness is impatience, so I am working on listening more carefully."
