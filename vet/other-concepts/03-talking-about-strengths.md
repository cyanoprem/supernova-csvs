Concept: Talk about Strengths
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence structure:
"My strength is [strength], which helps me [benefit]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. Your strength is teamwork.

Share how it benefits you.
2. Your strength is time management.

Share how it benefits you.
3. Your strength is communication.

Share how it benefits you.
4. Your strength is problem-solving.

Share how it benefits you.
5. Your strength is leadership.

Share how it benefits you.

Examples:
- "My strength is teamwork, which helps me collaborate well with others."
- "My strength is time management, which helps me complete tasks on schedule."
- "My strength is communication, which helps me explain ideas clearly."
- "My strength is problem-solving, which helps me find solutions quickly."
