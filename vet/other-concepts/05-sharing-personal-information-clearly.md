Concept: Sharing Personal Information Clearly
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"I [action] [detail]."

Questions:
1. Share where you live.
2. Share about your family.
3. Share what food you like.
4. Share what you do for fun.
5. Share where you studied.

Examples:
- "I live in Bangalore."
- "I have two younger sisters."
- "I love South Indian food."
- "I enjoy morning walks."
- "I studied in a government school."
- "I speak three languages."
