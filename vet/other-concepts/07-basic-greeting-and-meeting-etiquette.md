Concept: Basic Greeting and Meeting Etiquette
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence structure:
"Good [morning/afternoon]. Thank you for [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. You enter the interview room in the morning. The interviewer gave you this opportunity.

Greet them and thank them.
2. The interviewer welcomes you in the afternoon. They are giving you their time.

Greet them and thank them.
3. You meet the HR manager in the morning. They invited you for this interview.

Greet them and thank them.
4. You join an online interview in the afternoon. The panel is having you today.

Greet them and thank them.
5. You arrive for an interview in the morning. The company called you for this role.

Greet them and thank them.

Examples:
- "Good morning. Thank you for this opportunity."
- "Good afternoon. Thank you for having me today."
- "Good morning. Thank you for your time."
- "Good afternoon. Thank you for inviting me for this interview."
