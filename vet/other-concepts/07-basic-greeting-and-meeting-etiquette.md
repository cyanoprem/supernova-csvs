Concept: Basic Greeting and Meeting Etiquette
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"Good [morning/afternoon]. Thank you for [reason]."

Questions:
1. You enter the interview room in the morning.

Greet the interviewer and thank them.
2. The interviewer welcomes you to the interview.

Greet them and thank them.
3. You meet the HR manager in the afternoon.

Greet them and thank them.
4. You join an online interview in the morning.

Greet the panel and thank them.
5. The interviewer says "Please have a seat."

Greet them and thank them.

Examples:
- "Good morning. Thank you for this opportunity."
- "Good afternoon. Thank you for having me today."
- "Good morning. Thank you for your time."
- "Good afternoon. Thank you for inviting me for this interview."
