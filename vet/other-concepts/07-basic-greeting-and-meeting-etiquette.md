Concept: Basic Greeting and Meeting Etiquette
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"Good [morning/afternoon]. Thank you for [reason]."

Examples:
- "Good morning. Thank you for this opportunity."
- "Good afternoon. Thank you for having me today."
- "Good morning. Thank you for your time."
- "Good afternoon. Thank you for inviting me for this interview."
