Concept: Talking about Daily Routines
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"I usually [action] at [time]. After that, I [next action]."

Examples:
- "I usually wake up at 6 AM. After that, I exercise for 30 minutes."
- "I usually have breakfast at 7 AM. After that, I go to the office."
- "I usually finish work at 5 PM. After that, I spend time with my family."
- "I usually have dinner at 8 PM. After that, I read a book before sleeping."
