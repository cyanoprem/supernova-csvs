Concept: Describing People and Places
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"[Person/Place] is [adjective]. [He/She/It] has [feature]."

Examples:
- "Priya is stylish. She has fashionable dresses."
- "The library is spacious. It has comfortable seating."
- "The cafe is cozy. It has soft lighting."
- "My teacher is experienced. He has deep knowledge."
- "The office is modern. It has organized workspaces."
