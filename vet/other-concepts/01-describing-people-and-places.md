Concept: Describing People and Places
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence structure:
"[Person/Place] is [adjective]. [He/She/It] has [detail]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. Describe your best friend and share one detail about them.
2. Describe your college library and share one detail about it.
3. Describe your favourite teacher and share one detail about them.
4. Describe your city and share one detail about it.
5. Describe your classroom and share one detail about it.

Examples:
- "Priya is stylish. She has fashionable dresses."
- "Our library is spacious. It has comfortable seating."
- "The cafe is cozy. It has great lighting."
- "My teacher is experienced. He has deep knowledge."
- "The office is modern. It has organized workspaces."
