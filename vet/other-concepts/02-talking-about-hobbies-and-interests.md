Concept: Talking about Hobbies and Interests
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"I enjoy [hobby/interest] because [reason]. I do this [frequency]."

Examples:
- "I enjoy playing cricket because it keeps me fit. I do this every weekend."
- "I enjoy reading books because I learn new things. I do this in my free time."
- "I enjoy painting because it helps me express my creativity. I do this twice a week."
- "I enjoy cooking because I can make dishes for my family. I do this on Sundays."
