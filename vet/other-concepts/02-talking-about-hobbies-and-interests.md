Concept: Talking about Hobbies and Interests
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

Sentence structure:
"I enjoy [hobby/interest] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. What do you enjoy doing in your free time?

Share the reason.
2. What hobby do you enjoy the most?

Share the reason.
3. What activity do you enjoy on weekends?

Share the reason.
4. What do you enjoy doing to relax?

Share the reason.
5. What do you enjoy doing with friends or family?

Share the reason.

Examples:
- "I enjoy playing cricket because it keeps me fit."
- "I enjoy reading books because I learn new things."
- "I enjoy painting because it helps me be creative."
- "I enjoy cooking because I can make dishes for my family."
