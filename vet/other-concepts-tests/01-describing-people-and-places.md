Concept: Describing People and Places
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"[Person/Place] is [adjective]. [He/She/It] has [detail]."

Pre and Post Questions:
1. "Rahul is tall. He has a bright smile."
2. "My college is big. It has a large playground."
3. "Sneha is cheerful. She has a friendly nature."
4. "The canteen is busy. It has many food options."
5. "My father is hardworking. He has a calm voice."
6. "The park is green. It has tall trees."
7. "Arjun is smart. He has good grades."
8. "Our hostel is clean. It has big rooms."
9. "My mother is caring. She has a warm smile."
10. "The temple is old. It has beautiful carvings."
11. "Divya is creative. She has amazing drawing skills."
12. "The market is crowded. It has many small shops."
13. "My brother is funny. He has a great sense of humour."
14. "The hospital is new. It has modern equipment."
15. "Neha is quiet. She has a gentle voice."
16. "The bus stop is small. It has a tin roof."
17. "My grandfather is wise. He has years of experience."
18. "The school is old. It has a big garden."
19. "Vikram is athletic. He has a strong build."
20. "The restaurant is popular. It has delicious food."
