Concept: Talk about Weaknesses
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

Sentence structure:
"My weakness is [weakness], so I am working on [action]."

Pre and Post Questions:
1. "My weakness is shyness, so I am working on talking to new people."
2. "My weakness is overthinking, so I am working on making quick decisions."
3. "My weakness is procrastination, so I am working on starting tasks early."
4. "My weakness is saying no, so I am working on setting boundaries."
5. "My weakness is writing in English, so I am working on practising daily."
6. "My weakness is teamwork, so I am working on listening to others."
7. "My weakness is focus, so I am working on reducing distractions."
8. "My weakness is nervousness, so I am working on staying calm."
9. "My weakness is planning, so I am working on using a daily schedule."
10. "My weakness is multitasking, so I am working on doing one thing at a time."
11. "My weakness is speaking clearly, so I am working on my pronunciation."
12. "My weakness is confidence, so I am working on practising in front of a mirror."
13. "My weakness is asking for help, so I am working on reaching out to seniors."
14. "My weakness is deadlines, so I am working on managing my time better."
15. "My weakness is giving feedback, so I am working on being more honest."
16. "My weakness is reading speed, so I am working on reading every day."
17. "My weakness is presentation skills, so I am working on practising more."
18. "My weakness is handling criticism, so I am working on staying open-minded."
19. "My weakness is memory, so I am working on taking better notes."
20. "My weakness is group discussions, so I am working on sharing my views."
