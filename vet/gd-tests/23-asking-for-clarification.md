Concept: Group Discussion - Asking for Clarification
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"Could you explain what you mean by [term/phrase]?"

Pre and Post Questions:
1. "Could you explain what you mean by online shopping being risky?"
2. "Could you explain what you mean by social media spreading fake news?"
3. "Could you explain what you mean by work from home being less productive?"
4. "Could you explain what you mean by online learning not being effective?"
5. "Could you explain what you mean by internships being a waste of time?"
6. "Could you explain what you mean by women not getting equal opportunities?"
7. "Could you explain what you mean by virtual meetings being impersonal?"
8. "Could you explain what you mean by offline shopping being outdated?"
9. "Could you explain what you mean by face-to-face classes being costly?"
10. "Could you explain what you mean by gender roles changing slowly?"
11. "Could you explain what you mean by online shopping replacing stores?"
12. "Could you explain what you mean by social media creating pressure?"
13. "Could you explain what you mean by remote work hurting teamwork?"
14. "Could you explain what you mean by traditional classrooms being boring?"
15. "Could you explain what you mean by internships being only for big cities?"
16. "Could you explain what you mean by women facing bias at work?"
17. "Could you explain what you mean by video calls being tiring?"
18. "Could you explain what you mean by offline stores being expensive?"
19. "Could you explain what you mean by online courses lacking interaction?"
20. "Could you explain what you mean by work from office wasting time?"
