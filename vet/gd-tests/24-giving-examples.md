Concept: Group Discussion - Giving Examples
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"For example, [situation] because [reason]."

Pre and Post Questions:
1. "For example, Amazon delivers fast because it has many warehouses."
2. "For example, WhatsApp groups help students because they share notes."
3. "For example, Infosys offers work from home because employees prefer it."
4. "For example, NPTEL is popular because it has free college courses."
5. "For example, Tata hires interns because they find good candidates early."
6. "For example, Norway leads in gender equality because of strong laws."
7. "For example, Zoom became popular because people needed virtual meetings."
8. "For example, Reliance Trends does well because people like trying clothes."
9. "For example, Unacademy grew because students in villages got access."
10. "For example, Wipro takes interns because they build a talent pipeline."
11. "For example, Meesho grew because small sellers use social media."
12. "For example, Google allows flexible work because it trusts employees."
13. "For example, Khan Academy helps because lessons are free and simple."
14. "For example, startups take interns because they need fresh ideas."
15. "For example, Sweden supports working mothers because they offer childcare."
16. "For example, Microsoft Teams is used because offices went remote."
17. "For example, D-Mart is popular because prices are lower in store."
18. "For example, Coursera is useful because you get certificates online."
19. "For example, Deloitte hires interns because they train future employees."
20. "For example, Japan promotes women leaders because diversity helps business."
