Concept: Group Discussion - Adding to Someone's Point
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "I'd like to add that [new information]."
   Example: "I'd like to add that online shopping also offers more discounts during festivals like Diwali."
2. "To support this, [fact/statistic]."
   Example: "To support this, a recent survey showed that 70% of Indian women prefer working from home."
3. "For example, [specific example]."
   Example: "For example, many students in rural India got access to education through online learning during COVID."
