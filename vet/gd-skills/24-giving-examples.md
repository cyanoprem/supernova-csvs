Concept: Group Discussion - Giving Examples
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "For example, [what happened]."
   Example: "For example, many companies like TCS now allow employees to work from home three days a week."
2. "Take [famous case/company/person], [what happened]."
   Example: "Take Flipkart, they started as an online bookstore and changed how India shops."
3. "Imagine if [hypothetical situation], [what would happen]."
   Example: "Imagine if all schools switched to online learning, students in small towns could learn from the best teachers."
