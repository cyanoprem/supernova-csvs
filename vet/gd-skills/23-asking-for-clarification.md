Concept: Group Discussion - Asking for Clarification
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "Could you explain what you mean by [term/phrase]?"
   Example: "Could you explain what you mean by social media being a bane for students?"
2. "So you're saying [your understanding], is that correct?"
   Example: "So you're saying traditional learning is better than online learning, is that correct?"
3. "Can you give a specific example of [point]?"
   Example: "Can you give a specific example of how gender equality has improved in Indian workplaces?"
