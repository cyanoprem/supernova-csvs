Concept: Group Discussion - Concluding a Discussion
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "To summarize, the main points were [point 1], [point 2], and [point 3]."
   Example: "To summarize, the main points were that online shopping is convenient, offline shopping lets you check quality, and both have their place."
2. "It seems like we all agree that [shared conclusion]."
   Example: "It seems like we all agree that internships are important for career growth."
3. "To conclude, I believe [final opinion] because [strongest reason]."
   Example: "To conclude, I believe gender equality in the workplace is improving because more companies now have equal pay policies."
