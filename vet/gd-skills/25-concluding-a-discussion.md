Concept: Group Discussion - Concluding a Discussion
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"To summarize, the main points were [point 1], [point 2], and [point 3]."

Examples:
- "To summarize, the main points were that online shopping is convenient, offline shopping lets you check quality, and both have benefits."
- "To summarize, the main points were that internships teach skills, build experience, and help with career growth."
- "To summarize, the main points were that gender equality is improving, companies are changing, and there's still work to do."
