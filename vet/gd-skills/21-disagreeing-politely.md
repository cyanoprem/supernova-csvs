Concept: Group Discussion - Disagreeing Politely
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "I see your point, but I think [alternative view]."
   Example: "I see your point, but I think face-to-face meetings build stronger trust than virtual calls."
2. "I disagree about [part]."
   Example: "I disagree about social media being only harmful for students."
3. "Perhaps [alternative] would be a better approach because [reason]."
   Example: "Perhaps a mix of work from home and office would be a better approach because it gives flexibility and teamwork."
