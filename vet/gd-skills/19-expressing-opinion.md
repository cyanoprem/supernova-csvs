Concept: Group Discussion - Expressing Opinion
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"I think [opinion] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. Why do you think online shopping is more convenient? 

Give a reason?
2. Why do you think social media is helpful for people?

Give a reason?
3. Why do you think work from home is better?

Give a reason?
4. Why do you think online learning is useful for students?

Give a reason?
5. Why do you think internships are important?

Give a reason?

Examples:
- "I think online shopping is convenient because it saves time."
- "I think work from home is better because it gives more family time."
- "I think social media is helpful because it connects people."
- "I think internships are important because they give experience."
