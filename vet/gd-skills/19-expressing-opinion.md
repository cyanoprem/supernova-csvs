Concept: Group Discussion - Expressing Opinion
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"I think [opinion] because [reason]."

Examples:
- "I think online shopping is more convenient because you can buy anytime from home."
- "I think work from home is better because it gives more time with family."
- "I think social media is helpful because it connects people across the world."
- "I think internships are important because they give real-world experience."
