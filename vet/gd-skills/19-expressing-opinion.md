Concept: Group Discussion - Expressing Opinion
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "I think [statement]."
   Example: "I think online shopping saves more time than offline shopping."
2. "In my opinion [option A] is better because [reason]."
   Example: "In my opinion work from home is better because we save travel time."
3. "I suggest we [action]."
   Example: "I suggest we use both online and offline learning together."
