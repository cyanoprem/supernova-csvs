Concept: Group Discussion - Agreeing with Others
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do
Note: Use the exact roleplay questions below in the conversation roleplay section.

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"I agree with [name] because [reason]."

Roleplay Questions:
1. Priya says social media is useful.

Agree with Priya and give a reason.
2. Rahul says online learning is better.

Agree with Rahul and give a reason.
3. Ram says internships are valuable.

Agree with Ram and give a reason.
4. Sneha says work from home is easier.

Agree with Sneha and give a reason.
5. Arjun says face-to-face meetings are important.

Agree with Arjun and give a reason.

Examples:
- "I agree with Priya because social media helps businesses grow."
- "I agree with Rahul because online learning is more accessible."
- "I agree with Ram because internships give practical experience."
