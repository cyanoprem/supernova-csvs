Concept: Group Discussion - Agreeing with Others
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structure:
"I agree with [name] because [reason]."

Examples:
- "I agree with Priya because social media helps businesses reach more customers."
- "I agree with Rahul because online learning gives access to quality education everywhere."
- "I agree with Ram because internships give real-world experience that you can't get in college."
