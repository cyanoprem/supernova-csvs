Concept: Group Discussion - Agreeing with Others
Level: A1
Lesson type: Can Do

GD topics to use for sentences: Online Shopping vs. Offline Shopping, Social Media — Boon or Bane, Work From Home vs. Work From Office, Traditional Learning vs. Online Learning, Internship — Boon or Bane, Gender Equality, Face-to-Face vs. Virtual Communication, Women Development in India

Sentence structures:
1. "I agree with [name] because [reason]."
   Example: "I agree with Priya because social media does help small businesses grow."
2. "That's a good point, I've seen this in [context]."
   Example: "That's a good point, I've seen this in my college where online classes helped many students."
3. "Building on what [name] said, [extended point]."
   Example: "Building on what Rahul said, internships also teach us how to work in a team."
